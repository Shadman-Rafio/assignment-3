Ski Touring

Ski touring is a winter sport that combines elements of backcountry skiing and hiking. Participants use specialized skis with climbing skins attached to the base, allowing them to ascend slopes and explore remote, off-piste terrain. Unlike traditional downhill skiing, ski touring involves climbing uphill before descending, providing enthusiasts with a unique and immersive outdoor experience.
